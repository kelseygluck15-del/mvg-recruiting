Common Questions

Is this a scam? Why do you need my info?

Absolutely not. MVG Recruiting has been a trusted name in legal research for over 30 years. We collect demographic info only to ensure we have a diverse mix of people for our studies, as required by our clients. We never ask you for money; we pay you.

How do I know if I'm selected?

After you fill out a questionnaire, our coordinators review the responses. If you are a match, we will call or email you for a brief phone screening. Due to high volume, we only contact those who move to the next stage.

What happens during a "Mock Jury" session?

You will listen to presentations from real attorneys about an upcoming case. You'll then participate in a group discussion (deliberation) to provide your honest thoughts and feedback. Itâ€™s a fascinating look into the legal system!

What is the time commitment?

Most studies last between 4 to 8 hours. We always provide the exact timeframe in the study description before you sign up so you can plan your schedule accordingly.

How and when do I get paid?

Payment is usually issued immediately following the completion of the study. Depending on the project, this could be via check, digital payment (like Venmo or Zelle), or a pre-paid card.

Do I need legal experience?

No! In fact, we usually look for people who are NOT lawyers or legal professionals. We want the perspectives of everyday citizens from all walks of life.

What should I wear to a session?

Business casual is the standard. We want you to be comfortable but professional, as these sessions often take place in law offices or boardroom settings.

Is my information kept private?

Yes. Your personal contact information is used only by MVG coordinators. Your individual opinions during a study are kept confidential and are used by legal teams for research purposes only.
