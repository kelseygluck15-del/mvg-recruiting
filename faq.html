Common Questions

Is this a scam?

Absolutely not. We have been a trusted name in legal research for over 30 years. We never ask you for money; we pay you for your time.

How do I know if I'm selected?

If you match a study profile, a coordinator will reach out via phone or email for a brief screening interview.

What is a Mock Jury?

A mock jury is a research session where you listen to legal arguments and provide feedback to help attorneys prepare for trial.

How do I get paid?

Payment is issued immediately after the study via check, digital payment, or pre-paid card.

Do I need experience?

No. We want the perspectives of everyday citizens from all backgrounds.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your contact information is never sold and is used only for study recruitment purposes.
